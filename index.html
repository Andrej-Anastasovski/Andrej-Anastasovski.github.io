About Me

I’m a freelance web developer and graphic designer. My work focuses on creating responsive, functional websites and designing visuals that meet client needs. I combine technical skills with a design background to build websites that work well and look professional.

Before becoming a web developer, I spent over two years working as a graphic designer. I gained experience in creating logos, branding materials, and other designs for businesses. This gave me an understanding of how important design is for user experience, which I now use in my web development projects. My goal is to ensure that the websites I create are not only functional but also well-designed and easy to use.

In addition to my front-end focus, I have skills in Python, C#, and SQL, allowing me to take on full-stack projects when needed. I’m also pursuing a master’s degree in computer science with a focus on cybersecurity, which helps me approach projects with a deeper understanding of secure and scalable systems.

What I Do

Web Development: I specialize in front-end development, using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React. I focus on building responsive websites that work on different devices.

Graphic Design: I design logos, branding materials, and other visuals for businesses. I ensure that my designs align with the client’s brand and message.

Full-Stack Projects: I have the skills to handle back-end work using Python, C#, and SQL, delivering complete solutions for more complex projects.

Why Work With Me

Clear Communication: I keep clients informed throughout the project, ensuring their vision is understood and delivered.
